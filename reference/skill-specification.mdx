## What an epic contains

At minimum, an epic is:

- `SKILL.md` for discovery and activation
- `EPIC.md` for the durable operating definition

Most useful epics also grow some or all of:

- `runtime/plans/`
- `runtime/state.json` or `runtime/state/`
- `runtime/log/`
- `runtime/ROADMAP.md`
- `runtime/DECISIONS.md`
- `runtime/artifacts/`
- `hooks/`
- `cron.d/`
- `policy.yml`

## Essential rules at a glance

- `SKILL.md` and `EPIC.md` are the only required files.
- `runtime/state/core.json` is authoritative over `runtime/state.json` when
  split state is used.
- `runtime/plans/` carries the current tactical work.
- `runtime/log/` is append-only history.
- `policy.yml` is a hard constraint when present.
- Published epics may include `metadata.source`, but it remains optional.
